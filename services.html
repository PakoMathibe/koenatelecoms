Wireless LAN Architecture Design

At Koenatelecoms, we understand the importance of a robust wireless LAN (Local Area Network) for modern businesses. Our experts have extensive experience in designing and implementing wireless LAN architectures that provide reliable and high-performance connectivity. We consider factors such as coverage, capacity, security, and scalability to deliver wireless solutions tailored to meet specific business needs.

Unified Communications

We offer comprehensive unified communications solutions that integrate various communication channels, including voice, video, instant messaging, and collaboration tools. Our unified communications services enable businesses to enhance productivity, streamline communication processes, and facilitate efficient collaboration across teams and locations.

Microwave Backhaul PTP & PMP

Koenatelecoms specializes in microwave backhaul solutions, both point-to-point (PTP) and point-to-multipoint (PMP). We design and deploy microwave systems that provide high-speed, reliable, and secure data transmission for businesses. Our solutions are customized to meet specific bandwidth requirements, link distances, and environmental conditions, ensuring optimal performance.

Access and Connectivity

Koenatelecoms excels in providing robust access and connectivity solutions for businesses. We offer a wide range of services including broadband connectivity, wired and wireless network design, internet connectivity solutions, and network optimization. Our experts work closely with clients to understand their unique requirements and deliver tailored solutions that ensure seamless connectivity.

Switching and Routing

We specialize in designing and implementing efficient switching and routing solutions for networks of all sizes. Our team of skilled engineers helps businesses optimize their network infrastructure, improve performance, enhance security, and achieve seamless data transfer. We leverage industry-leading technologies to ensure reliable and scalable switching and routing solutions.

Turnkey Fiber Solutions

Fiber-optic networks are the backbone of modern telecommunications infrastructure. Koenatelecoms offers turnkey fiber solutions, including planning, design, installation, and maintenance of fiber-optic networks. We ensure the highest quality standards, enabling businesses to leverage the benefits of high-speed, scalable, and reliable connectivity.

Masts & Tower Builds

For businesses requiring infrastructure for wireless communications, Koenatelecoms provides mast and tower build services. Our team of experts manages the entire process, from site selection and design to installation and maintenance, ensuring safe and efficient deployment of wireless communication infrastructure.

LAN Services

Koenatelecoms offers a range of LAN (Local Area Network) services, including network design, implementation, maintenance, and optimization. Our LAN services help businesses create secure and efficient networks that support their daily operations and enable seamless data sharing and collaboration.
